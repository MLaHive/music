Temptress of Mankind

Feared. Condemned. Immortalized. Finally free.

scroll ↓

A descent through broken faith, false prophets, hollow worship, and transformation through fire. Temptress of Mankind follows the collapse of imposed belief and the rise of something feared, condemned, and finally empowered.

Each track tears away another layer of hypocrisy, exposing the machinery behind guilt, control, and manufactured salvation.

What begins as rebellion becomes revelation — and by the end, the villain they warned about stands fully awakened.
